Master The Supply Chain

You've probably heard a lot about the supply chain the past few years, and the pandemic-related shortages of everything from cars to produce to household goods.

Chicago is one of the world's largest supply chain hubs — and Elmhurst was the first in the area to offer and M.S. in Supply Chain Management.

Our part-time, 21-month program will give you the analytical know-how you need to optimize the supply chain for just about any kind of industry. Whether you attend online, on-campus, or a blend of both, you'll learn key skills for this fast-growing field, including:

  • Business and financial strategies
  • Benchmarking and forecasting
  • Outsourcing
  • Inbound logistics
  • Global logistics networks
  • Warehouse management
  • Inventory and packing strategies

At Elmhurst you're learning from experts. Our faculty have experience in strategic sourcing and purchasing, logistics, operations management, IT, e-commerce and customer relations.
